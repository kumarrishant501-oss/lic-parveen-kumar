Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about LIC insurance and our services

What is LIC and why should I choose it?
Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) is a Government of India enterprise and the largest insurance company in India. With over 65 years of trust, 100% claim settlement record, and government backing, LIC offers unmatched security and reliability for your insurance needs.
How do I choose the right insurance plan?
Choosing the right plan depends on your age, income, family responsibilities, and financial goals. As your advisor, I conduct a detailed needs analysis to recommend plans that perfectly match your requirements and budget. Contact me for a personalized consultation.
What documents are required for policy purchase?
Basic documents include identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN), address proof, age proof, income proof, passport photos, and bank details. For specific plans, additional documents may be required. I provide complete guidance on documentation requirements.
How long does policy approval take?
Policy approval typically takes 7-15 days depending on the plan type and medical requirements. For plans requiring medical examination, it may take slightly longer. I ensure all documentation is complete to expedite the process.
Can I pay premiums online?
Yes, LIC offers multiple convenient payment options including online payment through their website/app, auto-debit, NEFT/RTGS, and traditional methods. I assist you in setting up the most convenient payment method for your policy.
What support do you provide after policy purchase?
I provide comprehensive lifetime support including premium payment reminders, policy servicing assistance, claim settlement support, policy revival help, and guidance on policy loans or surrenders. Your satisfaction is my priority.
